§ 01 — About

A life lived
between pages

I am Isha Kansal, a writer, editor, and emerging academician currently pursuing my Master of Arts in English at Shiv Nadar University, Greater Noida. My academic journey has been shaped by a deep love for literature, cinema, and the craft of language itself.

I have authored two self-published books of poetry, Learning the Ways of Life and I Told the Stars About You, available on Amazon and Flipkart. I am interested in writing poetry, fiction, and critical essays.

Beyond writing, I have served as Chief Editor of The Freewheeler, worked as a Teaching Assistant at Shiv Nadar University, interned at Penguin Random House India and Dehradun Literature Festival and served as a campus ambassador for Teach For India (TFI). My experience working at diverse places has helped me enrich my skill set and learn from experts in different fields.

As a scholar, my research interests comprise Partition Literature, Women's writings, Film studies, and pedagogy.
